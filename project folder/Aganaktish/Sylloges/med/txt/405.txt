 1207. solitary mast cell granuloma (histiocytoma) of the lung. a histopathologic, tissue culture and time-lapse cinematographic study a pulmonary histiocytoma in a 57-year-old woman was studied intensively histopathologically and by other techniques. abundant mast cells were found within the lesion, a previously unreported finding. tissue culture studies suggested that the process is reactive rather than neoplastic. some plasma-cell granulomas of the lung may be mast cell histiocytomas.
